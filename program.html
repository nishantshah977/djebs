Programs Offered

Montessori

In this system of education, we enable the young children to develop their natural interests through various curricular activities rather than use formal teaching methods by the proper guidance and monitoring of experts. The Montessori method is a unique educational approach that enables children to learn at their own pace. The theory behind this educational philosophy is that children have a natural thirst for knowledge and develop skills as they acquire these bits of information over time.


Primary Level

At the younger age students may have a hard time understanding the concepts they are learning. They are also not yet able to read, write and recite. They may have a hard time remembering certain facts that they studied earlier in their lives. In this stage, we provide the young children a basic understanding of various subjects as well as the skills such as reading, writing, spelling, interpersonal communication which they could use throughout their lives through various curricular activities.


Basic Level

In this stage, kids understand and memorize the fundamental concepts of various subjects. They are shown setups of basic objects, machines and concepts needed to understand the subject in depth. They are also encouraged to develop their own studies, research and sketches based on the explanations given by experts.


Secondary Level

Students are trained for the competitive world by providing them modern curriculum based course by our skilled faculties. At First Level, students are taught various concepts of commerce, mathematics and basic sciences. This is followed by interdisciplinary studies which includes soft skills such as communication, presentation and teamwork. With a goal of preparing you for life after secondary education, we offer a variety of skills training programs that cover Maths, English and IT.
